Request for Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your account and associated data from our application, please follow the instructions below:

  1. Email us at hs.umarov21@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request".
  2. In the email, please include:
    • Your registered phone number
    • A brief description of your request
  3. Our support team will process your request within 7 business days.

Data Deletion and Retention Information

Upon receiving your request, we will delete the following data:

  • Personal information (full name, phone number)
  • Account activity logs

The following data will be retained for legal and administrative purposes for a period of up to 30 days after account deletion:

  • Transaction records
  • Communication logs with support

For any further queries, please contact our support at hs.umarov21@gmail.com.
